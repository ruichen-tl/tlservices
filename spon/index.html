Our Background

An initiative of TalentLift Canada: Merging technology with human rights law.

TalentLift Canada is a registered charity and a recognized Law Society Civil Society Organization (CSO). Over years of processing highly intricate, life-critical immigration files at scale, we have engineered robust digital frameworks that prioritize absolute data accuracy and privacy protection.

Our leadership team consists of practicing immigration lawyers and data systems architects who understand exactly how crucial details are when dealing with IRCC reviews. We design software tools optimized to eliminate operational risks, ensuring your personal relationship history and official records are prepared to standard.
