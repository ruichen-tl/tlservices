The Challenge

The final step shouldn't be the most confusing one.

Physical Presence Math

To qualify after your 3 years (1,095 days), calculating exact travel dates, partial days spent as a temporary resident, or time away can become an error-prone headache that triggers severe processing delays.

Checklist Complexity

Providing valid language proofs, tracking down old tax filings, and matching up employment history back-to-back leaves zero room for gaps or unexplained periods without triggering an IRCC file return.

Multiplied Family Fees

Law firms regularly charge full, separate legal fees for every individual member of a family group, turning what should be a shared celebration into a restrictive financial barrier.
